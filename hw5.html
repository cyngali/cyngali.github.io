Yoga for the soul

How yoga can benefit you

For starters, yoga is an amazing form of physical excersise that improves strength, felxibility, and balance.
The practice of slow movements and deep breathing allows the increase of blood flow while holding a certain yoga pose can build stregth. Some physical benefits include: relieving back pain, easing arthritis symptoms, and overall improving heart health.

In addition, yoga can help aid your stress levels. It is a great tool that supports stress mamagement as well as mental health.
The breathing techniques accompanied by the various poses promote mindfulness. This can improve eating patterns and quality of sleep. Overall, yoga is a recommended excersise that does wonders for ones mental health.
